Saltrice Porridge

Hi! Today I will teach you how to make saltrice porridge, this is a very simple dish from the land of Skyrim, the main ingredient of the dish is Saltrice which you can find in almost every field across Skyrim!

Ingredients required

  • ¾ cup white rice
  • 1½
  • 2½ cups milk
  • 1-2 Tablespoons honey
  • 2 Tablespoons vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Various toppings of your choice
